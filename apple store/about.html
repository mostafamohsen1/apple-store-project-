About Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company that revolutionized the technology sector through its innovation of computer software, personal computers, mobile tablets, smartphones, and computer peripherals.

Shop Now

Our History

Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple began as a personal computer pioneer. The company's journey from a garage startup to one of the world's most valuable brands is a testament to innovation and vision.

Apple introduced revolutionary products like the Macintosh in 1984, the iPod in 2001, the iPhone in 2007, and the iPad in 2010, each redefining their respective markets and setting new standards for technology and design.

Our Mission

To bring the best user experience to customers through innovative hardware, software, and services. Apple is committed to designing products that are not only powerful and intuitive but also beautiful and environmentally responsible.

Our Values

Apple is guided by a set of core values that define our company culture and shape our products:

  • Innovation: We believe in the power of innovation to change the world.
  • Quality: We are committed to excellence in everything we create.
  • Simplicity: We strive for elegant simplicity in design and user experience.
  • Privacy: We are dedicated to protecting our users' privacy and data.
  • Sustainability: We aim to leave the world better than we found it.
